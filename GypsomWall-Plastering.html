Gypsum Wall Plastering

Gypsum wall plastering is a widely used technique for finishing interior walls, offering a smooth and aesthetically pleasing surface. This method utilizes gypsum plaster, which is a mixture of gypsum powder and water, applied directly to the walls to create a durable and attractive finish.
